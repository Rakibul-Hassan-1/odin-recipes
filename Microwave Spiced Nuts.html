Microwave Spiced Nuts


This is a great microwave candied nut recipe I stole from my mom. She likes walnuts, and I like pecans. It's really easy. My wife says she needs to leave the house when I make them so she won't eat them all!




Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamonr
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons water
